History

Tricom Dynamics Inc. (TDI), founded in 1991 as part of the Tricom Group of Companies, has been a trusted partner in delivering innovative ICT solutions to empower Filipino businesses. With a comprehensive portfolio that includes TOSHIBA e-studio MFPs, Dynabook laptops, Viewsonic displays, and Hikvision commercial systems, TDI provides not just top-tier products but also expert support from selection to after-sales service, ensuring exceptional client satisfaction.

Originating from Tricom Systems (Philippines), Inc., established in 1989 with a vision to revolutionize the country's telecommunications and office automation industry, Tricom expanded its impact through strategic partnerships and diversification. From installing over a thousand telecommunication units to offering complex connectivity solutions, Tricom has become synonymous with reliable service, supporting clients nationwide through service centers in Cebu, Davao, and Cagayan de Oro, and continuing to evolve as a comprehensive solutions provider.
